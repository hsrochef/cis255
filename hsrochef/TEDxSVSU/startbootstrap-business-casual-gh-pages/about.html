Ticket Information TEDxSVSU 2017

Call (989) 964-4295. Tickets are available now, but seats are very limited. Only 100 tickets will be sold in total. Whether you pay by check or by credit card, please call to confirm your seat. General admission ticket prices are $25 for attendees. Seating is free for volunteers, speakers, exhibitors and sponsors.
